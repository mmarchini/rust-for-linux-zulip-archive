Benno Lossin (Dec 05 2024 at 22:51):

you should be able to use concat! instead (and also normal doc parts for the stuff that doesn't require interpolation):

#[doc = concat!("Convert a [`", stringify!($opaque), "`] into its fully qualified [`", stringify!($full), "`]")]
///
#[doc = concat!("This is an infallible version of [`", stringify!($full), "::try_from_opaque`]. This function is")]
...
/// # Panics
///
...
