Topic: SpinLockIrq + CondVar is broken

Daniel Almeida (Mar 31 2025 at 12:30):

I've discussed with Alice and Lyude before, but I think @Boqun Feng was left out of the loop until now.

Using CondVar with SpinLockIrq does not work, it results in a lot of lockdep splats instead.

The issue is that do_unlocked() releases the guard, but not the IrqDisabled token:

        // SAFETY: Switches to another thread. The timeout can be any number.
        let ret = guard.do_unlocked(|| unsafe { bindings::schedule_timeout(timeout_in_jiffies) });

This means that we go to sleep with IRQs still disabled.

Alice sketched out a solution for this, but she's on leave currently.
