Danilo Krummrich (Oct 15 2024 at 21:51):

Yeah, the patch @Boqun Feng linked in is a good explanation.

The biggest difficulty is that FromIterator (which is required for a generic collect to properly work) doesn't handle GFP flags and allocation failures.

The GFP stuff may be solvable with PF_MEMALLOC_* flags in the long term, but infallibility is probably too heavy to lift.

I think for the long term we have to come up with a kernel specific FromIterator trait for collections to implement.
