Boqun Feng (Jul 15 2023 at 21:32):

rust-dev is not documented since it's currently not a mandatory step in the patch workflow: it's currently used as a patch queue for LGTM patches in case someone wants to develop/test with these patches. But it shouldn't affect anyone's development process, i.e. if you have a conflict with rust-dev, it doesn't matter. And the branch is volatile and subject to changes.
