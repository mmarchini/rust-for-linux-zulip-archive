Pratham Patel (Oct 19 2023 at 15:41):

I've been burnt by running make rustavailable and then being unable to find "Rust support" under "General setup" many times, until I realized that the march support wasn't enabled yet. This is in the context of experimenting on arm64 and riscv64 SBCs. Yes, I now realize that it needs to be enabled per-arch, but didn't know about it earlier and thought that it was already done.

I doubt CONFIG_RUST=y will be in the defconfig for a few years. Until then, maybe we should let the curious minds know _why_ Rust is _available_ but can't be enabled.

What I propose for this:

Require a .config file for make rustavailable to work. Though this check can be in the beginning, I think it's best if it is done _after_ all checks complete without errors. There, we can check the .config file for anything that is interfering with CONFIG_RUST=y.

I am creating this thread (forgot what Zulip calls it) to get a concensus on what maintainers want and will draft my RFC on the GitHub issue tracker appropriately. So, what are your thoughts?
