Miguel Ojeda (Jul 17 2023 at 22:23):

Yeah, we have been discussing lately how to give a better overview of everything that is going on. Patches are a big part of it, and we are looking into patchwork as an improvement for that as well as other possibilities. Nevertheless, subscribing to the mailing list is recommended! Perhaps with a filter to put it into a tag/folder and then reading it from time to time -- it is not very high traffic so far.

There are also quite a few other things going on usually (i.e. apart from the patches), and it would be nice to cover some more of that (at least at a high-level), if possible, at https://rust-for-linux.com.

We also have the "open meeting" back now again, which we encourage everybody to join since it is a great way to know more about what is going on! If there is enough demand for it, we could increase its frequency too.
