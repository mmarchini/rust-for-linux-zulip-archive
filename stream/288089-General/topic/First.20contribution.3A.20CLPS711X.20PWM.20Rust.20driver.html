Onur Özkan (Feb 15 2026 at 07:29):

The typical review process on the kernel is that you find the maintainers for the subtree you are changing. You send your patch to them and the public mailing list. You can get all the addresses by using the get_maintainer.pl script. The response time depends on the current workload of the maintainers, but usually you start to get some feedbacks in a couple of days.
