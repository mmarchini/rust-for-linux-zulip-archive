Boqun Feng (Nov 18 2022 at 03:33):

@Gary Guo mentioned about some difficulties of sending patches with SMTP.

Actually there is already a tool allows sending patches without SMTP/MTA (still in experiment). For those who are interested, the tool is

https://pypi.org/project/b4/

and I believe the following two options of b4 send are for web patch submission

--web-auth-new
--web-auth-verify VERIFY_TOKEN

For more information, the author describe the workflow at: https://lore.kernel.org/tools/20220902191622.rrcbq2obitnumfdb@nitro.local/
