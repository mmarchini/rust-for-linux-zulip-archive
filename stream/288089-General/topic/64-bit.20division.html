Alice Ryhl (Aug 26 2025 at 14:38):

There have been quite a few patches related to 64-bit division. Could we instead provide a definition of __aeabi_uldivmod in terms of div64_s64?

Christian Schrefl (Aug 26 2025 at 15:01):

From my understanding:
Yes we could technically do that, but we don't want to since this operation is very expensive on 32 bit architectures (and a full 64 bit by 64 bit division is usually not needed).

Implementing __aeabi_uldivmod would make this operation implicit and easy to use accidentally where it is not actually needed. It wasn't implemented in the first place because of this, since the same applies to C.
