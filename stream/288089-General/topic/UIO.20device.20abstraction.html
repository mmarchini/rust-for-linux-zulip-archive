Danilo Krummrich (Dec 12 2024 at 15:59):

I suggest to have a look at Nova, which does the same thing, just with a DRM instead of UIO device.

What I'm doing there is, put all the driver private data (including the Devres<pci::Bar>) into the DRM device and then put the DRM device into the PCI private driver data [1].

Note that a DRM device is reference counted, so the lifetime of the DRM device is not bound on the PCI private driver data. Also note that data is an Arc because a DRM device is allowed to outlive the driver.

[1] https://gitlab.freedesktop.org/drm/nova/-/blob/nova-next/drivers/gpu/drm/nova/driver.rs?ref_type=heads
