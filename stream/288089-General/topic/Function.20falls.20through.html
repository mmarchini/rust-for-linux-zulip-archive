Miguel Ojeda (Feb 07 2025 at 11:53):

Not sure what you mean by flags.

Typically that warning is objtool not knowing about a function being noreturn at the end of the symbol, so it thinks it falls through.

Normally, it is just a matter of adding the right symbol to the list (or a pattern) so that the tool can understand the control flow.
