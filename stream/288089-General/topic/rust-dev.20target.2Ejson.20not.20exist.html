Boqun Feng (Aug 15 2023 at 14:07):

Some explanation, in rust-dev branch, the ARM64 support patch is queued there, and it does

ifeq($(ARCH), x86_64))
always-$(CONFIG_RUST)                   += target.json
endif

However, even on a 64bit machine, if you just do make LLVM=1, ARCH will be x86 instead of x86_64 (see how SUBARCH is calculated).
