Alice Ryhl (Jul 22 2026 at 09:07):

You probably want to reserve the entry by calling xa_alloc() with XA_ZERO_ENTRY and then overwrite it with the real pointer during the critical region to do that then. Actually pre-allocating so that a call to xa_alloc() can succeed requires quite a few allocations that you will almost never need, so it's usually not done that way.
