Wedson Almeida Filho (Apr 06 2023 at 18:23):

Parts of the sync crate are being upstreamed now: https://lore.kernel.org/lkml/20230405175111.5974-1-wedsonaf@gmail.com/

They depend on y86's pin-init API: https://lore.kernel.org/lkml/20230405193445.745024-1-y86-dev@protonmail.com/

Where you should base your changes depends partially on when you'd like to upstream them: if soon, you should probably target rust-next. If you tell us what dependencies you have that are missing, we could take this into consideration when prioritising what is upstreamed first.

If you're not in a hurry, it's probably ok to work off of rust and wait for dependencies to make it to rust-next. We should note, however, that we are taking this opportunity to fix some issues, reconsider names, etc. So when we upstream things, they may not be exactly what we currently have in rust, so you may need to update your code.
