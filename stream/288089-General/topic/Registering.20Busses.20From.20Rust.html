Greg KH (Jan 08 2026 at 06:32):

I would love to see ALL of the hyperv drivers move over to rust (and told the MS developers in person this years ago). We have no problem dropping existing .c drivers for .rs drivers if the maintainers of the subsystem agree to, so work with them to make this happen.

That being said, it's probably easier to do a rust binding for the hyperv bus first, redo the drivers, and then consider the bus conversion itself, that's the most minor part of all of that work.

Good luck!
