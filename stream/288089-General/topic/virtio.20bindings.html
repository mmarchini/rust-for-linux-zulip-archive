Andreas Hindborg (Apr 23 2026 at 19:43):

Demi Obenour said:

Are most APIs fairly straightforward to wrap, or are they going to have nasty problems wrapping safely?

That question is difficult to answer. Some stuff is easy, some stuff requires more thought. Some times when we build an API with a specific use in mind, they do not work for other users. It can be tricky to figure out how to design APIs so that they are safe, intuitive to the user, and cover all users. Often we don't get it right in first try. It is mostly an iterative process.

But some things are more straight forward.
