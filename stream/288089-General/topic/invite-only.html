Why are we invite only? Is there a reason not to open it up?

Miguel Ojeda (Jun 10 2022 at 16:43):

It isn't really "invitation only", but rather "just request it", i.e. all invitations are accepted right away. When I created the Zulip, I wanted to have it so that anybody could request an invitation in the join form, but that did not seem to be an option. Since I didn't know if spam was/would be a problem with Zulip, I decided to put the "request invitation" text in GitHub's README. But it is probably fine opening it up too.

One nice thing I found about that system is that people sending the email usually introduce themselves -- I enjoy reading those. I also take the opportunity to ask if they are joining on behalf of an organization or on their own.
