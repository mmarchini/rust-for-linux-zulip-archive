Boqun Feng (May 30 2025 at 16:05):

For your last question, ArcBorrow is just a "borrow" of an object in Arc, you can get an Arc from ArcBorrow by deref() + .into(), which means increase the reference count (i.e. the callers hold the refcount themselves). This is probably only reasonable ArcBorrow -> Arc "conversion". And I'm not sure getting rid of the deref() part can provide any benefit.
