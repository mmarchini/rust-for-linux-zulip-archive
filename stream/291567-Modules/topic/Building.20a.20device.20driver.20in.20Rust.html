Martin Rodriguez Reboredo (Jan 02 2024 at 15:54):

There's a pair of things to consider when developing USB drivers, one is that generally you don't write an USB driver directly, you instead write drivers for other subsystems that are on top of it, like hid, input or usb-serial

Martin Rodriguez Reboredo (Jan 02 2024 at 15:56):

If I'd to submit my USB bindings it will only be bindings for USB interfaces and devices, drivers are kinda pointless due to the previous point

Martin Rodriguez Reboredo (Jan 02 2024 at 15:58):

Also the USB driver interface is not very much useful by itself, but other subsystems make good use of it
