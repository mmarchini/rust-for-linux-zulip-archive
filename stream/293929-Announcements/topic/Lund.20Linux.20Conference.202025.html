Andreas Hindborg (Dec 20 2024 at 11:39):

The Lund Linux Conference 2025 has been announced, see below. LLC is nice venue, so if you are in the neighborhood, consider joining :) Rust has been on the agenda for the past three years or so.

Hi all!

We're happy to inform you that LLC 2025 is on and that we are looking
forward to seeing you all again the 27th-28th of May.

Same procedure as previous years: the event will be kernel &
local-community-focused and completely free (as in free beverages AND
free and open source software).

The formal invitation with a registration link will be sent out by the
end of February.

We encourage you to contribute. If you have an idea for a talk that
you would like to give or if you would like to demo something, please
let us know via the contribution form. The talk format is either a 30
min presentation + 15 min Q&A or a 5-, 10- or 15-min lightning talk in
English. We are happy to help out if you want feedback on your
presentations, and let us know if you would like to discuss an idea -
we'd love to do this.

Contribution form: https://forms.gle/6yx3QgiEVyBn8WvH9

The topic of your talk should be something that falls within the
kernel hacker interest sphere of technical nature. If you're unsure,
let's discuss it!.

* Please send in your talk proposals by March 23rd, and please
describe the talk as well as you can - we need a good description to
plan the best possible program *

If you or your employer would like to sponsor the event, we have a
list of things we need help with. Drop us an email and we can take it
from there.

This invitation is personal, but if you know someone who might want to
join, point them to Lund Linux Con website where there is more
information, as well as a form for people to suggest themselves to be
invited:

Interest form: https://forms.gle/hEZP4Sz39V7AZRMR8

We're really happy to do this again, and we're looking forward to
meeting you all.
For any questions, don't hesitate to contact us @
lund-linuxcon-crew@googlegroups.com

Happy holidays and hope to see you in the new year!

/ LLC Crew
https://lundlinuxcon.org/
