Philipp Stanner (Mar 26 2026 at 16:33):

I tend to believe that the best data structure for storing pointers to the DmaFences you want to signal in the dma::FenceCtx would be an XArray.

Reason: you signal fences in order, and you often signal batches. Thus, you want something cache-friendly that gives you a for-each.

Agreed, @Boris Brezillon ?
