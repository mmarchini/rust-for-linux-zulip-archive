Topic: It's now impossible to impl WorkItem for drm::Device::data

Daniel Almeida (Jun 25 2025 at 14:50):

This field is no longer reference counted, so it cannot be used as WorkItem::Pointer. The trait cannot be implemented for drm::device::Device<T> or ARef<drm::device::Device<T>> either, because of the orphan rule. This leads to two issues:

a) We now have to build an extra indirection just to make this work, i.e.: we have to place inner members of drm::device::Device::data behind an Arc<T> just to share them with the worker, even though ARef<drm::device::Device<T>> is already ref-counted.

b) We now have to group fields into the same struct to place them in said Arc. This is just so that they can be accessed by the worker, even though they may be unrelated otherwise, i.e.:

struct DriverData {
  // this is clunky and unnecessary
  shared_worker_fields: Arc<SharedDriverData>,
  ...
}

c) different workers may need to access different fields, so eventually lots of things will end up in the SharedDriverData struct of the previous example

This looks like a regression (feature-wise) from what we had before.
