Miguel Ojeda (Oct 20 2024 at 16:39):

Regarding third-party crates, please see https://rust-for-linux.com/third-party-crates.

Regarding the docs, https://rust-for-linux.github.io/docs/kernel/ is just an archive of old docs -- there is a red banner there that points you to the current one, which is what is in the mainline kernel. Please see https://rust-for-linux.com/branches#rust as well if you want to understand more of the context.

I hope that helps!
