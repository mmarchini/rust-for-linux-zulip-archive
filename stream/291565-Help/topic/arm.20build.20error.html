Miguel Ojeda (Oct 10 2023 at 09:14):

Why is float calculation blocked?
for exynos processor --target armv7a-none-eabihf is better.

Do you need floating point support in the kernel for your use case?

Clang appears to use gnueabi, see scripts/Makefile.clang:

CLANG_TARGET_FLAGS_arm      := arm-linux-gnueabi

And in CFLAGS_ABI they do:

CFLAGS_ABI  += -meabi gnu

They also enable -msoft-float and conditionally -mfpu=vfp etc. I would suggest trying to keep things as close as possible to what they do.

Do you expect arm/Makefile manage flag?

If you mean in general, yes, the arch's Makefile should ideally be the one managing the flags, like they do for C.
