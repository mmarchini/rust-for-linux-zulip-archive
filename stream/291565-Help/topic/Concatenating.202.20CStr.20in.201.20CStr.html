Alice Ryhl (Apr 19 2024 at 10:38):

Without that patchset, the methods you want are indeed the try_* methods. With the patchset, the answer is different. It provides methods called push and so on that take gfp flags as an argument.

Alice Ryhl (Apr 19 2024 at 10:40):

As for the format! macro, what you should use there is CString::try_from_fmt from rust/kernel/str.rs. The documentation for that method has some examples for how to use it.

Alice Ryhl (Apr 19 2024 at 10:44):

But as a general note, anything in the alloc crate that does allocations without giving you the opportunity to handle allocation failures will not be usable in the kernel.
