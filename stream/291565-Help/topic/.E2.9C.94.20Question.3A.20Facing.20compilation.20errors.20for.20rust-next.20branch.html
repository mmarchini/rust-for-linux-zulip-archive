Miguel Ojeda (Oct 20 2025 at 01:08):

Yeah, it is unrelated to Rust. If you can, please report it to the LKML. Meanwhile, if you want to ignore those errors, you could disable OBJTOOL_WERROR in the configuration, but it is not recommended. Another option is to disable those modules if you don't need them.

BluesMan (Oct 20 2025 at 01:22):

Thank you Miguel for your quick reply. I have found a workaround. Instead of copying the dot-config from /boot I have executed

make LLVM=1 localmodconfig

That did the trick.

AS arch/x86/boot/header.o
LD arch/x86/boot/setup.elf
OBJCOPY arch/x86/boot/setup.bin
BUILD arch/x86/boot/bzImage
kernel: arch/x86/boot/bzImage is ready (#1)

user 63m23.288s
sys 5m16.746s
