bjorn3 (Feb 13 2025 at 17:18):

Gdb should be able to handle mangling. You do have to set a breakpoint on my_crate::my_function (or my_crate::some_module::my_function if your function is inside mod some_module {}) rather than just my_function though. b path/to/my/file.rs:1234 should also work for setting a breakpoint on a specific line.

bjorn3 (Feb 13 2025 at 17:20):

Also unrelated to breakpoints, but you may want to use the rust-gdb wrapper that ships with rustc. This adds a bunch of pretty printers for rust types like slices.
