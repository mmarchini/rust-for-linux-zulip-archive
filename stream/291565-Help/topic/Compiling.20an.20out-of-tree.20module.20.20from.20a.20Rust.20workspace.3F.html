Miguel Ojeda (Apr 06 2024 at 11:09):

By Rust workspace, do you mean a Cargo one? i.e. you want to have it as a crate together with your other crates and compile it with Cargo?

We support building out-of-tree modules the usual way the kernel supports it, so you could perhaps create a crate in your workspace and use build.rs to invoke the compilation of your out-of-tree module, i.e. calling make. Please see https://rust-for-linux.com/out-of-tree-modules for some pointers, including a basic template of an out-of-tree module.

I hope that helps!
