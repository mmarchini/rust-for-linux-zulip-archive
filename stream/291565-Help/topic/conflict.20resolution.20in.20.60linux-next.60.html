Miguel Ojeda (Jul 11 2025 at 14:07):

Take a look at the options on how to show diffs for merges: https://git-scm.com/docs/git-show#Documentation/git-show.txt---diff-mergesformat

In particular, the --remerge-diff one shows how Stephen resolved the conflict today by showing you how he removed the markers and one of the sides entirely (since the line modified in rust-next is not needed anymore).
