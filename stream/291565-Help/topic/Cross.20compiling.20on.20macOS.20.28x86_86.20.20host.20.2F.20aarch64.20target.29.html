bjorn3 (Jun 26 2023 at 09:37):

Weird. If a crate can't be loaded as non-proc-macro first, rustc should try again to load it as proc-macro.

alxdrl (Jun 26 2023 at 10:19):

@bjorn3 well ... the is_proc_macro is set to false in rustc_metadata::locator and rejection happens in crate_matched() ... I don't understand how it's supposed to work but I'm kind of a noob there :-)

bjorn3 (Jun 26 2023 at 10:21):

That is the default value. load_proc_macro sets it to true. maybe_resolve_crate and thus resolve_crate will first call load and then if it fails call load_proc_macro.

alxdrl (Jun 26 2023 at 10:27):

thanks, I'll follow this lead

alxdrl (Jun 26 2023 at 22:03):

@bjorn3 ok, well of course on macOS the locator is looking for .dylib suffix, I just had to rename libmacros.so to libmacros.dylib and now I get further. Need to fix expected u64, found usize error now :-) Thanks again.

bjorn3 (Jun 26 2023 at 22:04):

Of course. That makes sense.

alxdrl (Jun 26 2023 at 22:26):
