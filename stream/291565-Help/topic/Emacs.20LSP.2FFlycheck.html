Andreas Hindborg (Feb 21 2025 at 11:30):

I have a patched version of b4 that allows you to set a prefix for the subject line like so:

b4 shazam -p LIST 20250210-rust-analyzer-bindings-include-v2-2-23dff845edc3@gmail.com

Then it will be applied to your tree with a prefix. Like here I applied some patches enabling werror for clippy below my hrtimer tree:
