Fabien Parent (May 30 2024 at 01:37):

It looks like you are assuming that everything from linux/tty.h would end up in a submodule tty of the bindings crate, but that's not the case.
Everything from linux/tty.h will be in bindings::. For instance the tty_struct should be accessible like this: bindings::tty_struct.
