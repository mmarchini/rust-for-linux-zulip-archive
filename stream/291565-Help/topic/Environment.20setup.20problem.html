Aswin Unnikrishnan (May 24 2024 at 18:29):

In menu config , type / and search for RUST . It’ll show you what the dependent options are and their current values. I often found that having loadable modules = Y prevents rust from being enabled.

Tommy Gilligan (May 25 2024 at 08:07):

i had a similar issue... had to resolve config dependencies. in my case i had to disable module versioning. ./scripts/config -d MODVERSIONS

Tommy Gilligan (May 26 2024 at 09:37):

you might also want to try

./scripts/config -d RANDSTRUCT VERSIONS
./scripts/config -d GCC_PLUGINS
./scripts/config -d DEBUG_INFO_BTF
