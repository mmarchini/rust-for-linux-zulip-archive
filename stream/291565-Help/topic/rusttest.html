Miguel Ojeda (Jul 07 2021 at 11:18):

Douglas Su said:

To my understanding, rusttest is running in userspace, and be managed by Cargo.

No, it is not managed by Cargo. We use Cargo to build a sysroot with the custom alloc crate because it is the "official" way of building std etc. But the actual compiling and running of the tests happens with Kbuild as usual.

Miguel Ojeda (Jul 07 2021 at 11:20):
