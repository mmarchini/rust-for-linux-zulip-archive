Benno Lossin (Feb 14 2024 at 11:16):

UnsafeCell is a low level primitive and should not be directly used. Also note that UnsafeCell is not Sync, so you will not be able to share Arc<UnsafeCell<T>> between threads, making it rather pointless to use an Arc.
